Read. Rate. Share.

For a real book lover, his favorite hobby is not limited to reading alone. In our free time from bright adventures, philosophical thoughts and tearful dramas, we buy new copies for our collection of books, share our impressions of what we have read, look for something to add to our reading list, and follow the novelties on the book shelves. And although beautiful notebooks are the best friends of avid readers, it is still more convenient to work with information in electronic format. Discover the world of literature, find new books and write down your impressions!
